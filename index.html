What We Do

Our Services

Focused engagements that close the gap between planning data and business performance.

Demand Forecasting

Improve statistical forecast accuracy using your existing ERP and planning data. We diagnose bias, reduce error, and implement forecasting models that reflect real demand patterns — seasonality, promotions, and lifecycle included.

  • Higher MAPE / WMAPE performance
  • Reduced stockouts and overstock events
  • Better alignment between sales and supply plans

Inventory Optimization

Right-size stock levels across your network. We model safety stock, reorder points, and replenishment logic to balance service levels against working capital — without over-relying on gut feel or spreadsheets.

  • Reduced excess and obsolete stock
  • Improved service fill rates
  • Capital freed from slow-moving inventory

Supply Chain Analytics

Turn raw ERP and planning data into actionable insight. We design KPI frameworks, build reporting structures, and identify the root causes of performance gaps — giving leadership clear visibility across the supply chain.

  • Consolidated planning KPI dashboards
  • Root-cause analysis of supply failures
  • Data-driven planning governance

ERP Planning Advisory

Get more from SAP, Microsoft Dynamics 365, or similar platforms. We optimise planning module configuration, master data quality, and process workflows so your ERP investment delivers real planning performance — not just system compliance.

  • Better ERP planning module utilisation
  • Cleaner master data and parameter setup
  • Faster, more reliable planning cycles

How We Work

A Practical Consulting Approach

Every engagement follows a structured path from problem clarity to measurable improvement.

  1. 01

    Diagnose

    We start by understanding your current planning environment — data quality, ERP setup, process maturity, and performance gaps. No assumptions. No generic frameworks applied before we understand what's actually happening.

  2. 02

    Design

    Based on diagnosis, we co-design a targeted improvement plan covering forecasting models, inventory policies, analytics structure, or ERP configuration — with clear objectives and measurable outcomes.

  3. 03

    Implement

    We work alongside your team to implement changes — configuring parameters, building models, structuring data, training planners, and embedding new processes into your existing workflows.

  4. 04

    Improve

    We track KPIs and review outcomes against targets. Performance monitoring, root-cause review, and iterative adjustment ensure improvements hold and continue to develop over time.
